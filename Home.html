PACMAN HISTORY

Play Pacman for free here. Pacman is an all time classic video game. When it was released in 1980, the popular games were Asteroids and Space Invaders. Pacman broke the mold, appealing to both men and women alike.

Use the arrow keys to move, avoid the ghosts. This game is made of 5 levels at each level the there is a different maze you should eat all of the fruits to win the game and rise up to the next level
